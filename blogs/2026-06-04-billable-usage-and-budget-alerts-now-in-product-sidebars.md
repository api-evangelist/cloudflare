Cloudflare has added billable usage tracking and budget alert creation directly to product sidebar widgets for pay-as-you-go customers across Workers, D1, R2, KV, Queues, Vectorize, Durable Objects, and Containers. The new sidebar widget shows current-period spend and billing cycle dates alongside a button to create budget alerts, eliminating the need to navigate away from product pages to monitor costs.
