Cloudflare Radar now displays traffic charts with improved granularity for longer time periods, providing daily granularity for 1-3 month views and weekly data for periods exceeding 3 months. This replaces the previous weekly and monthly aggregations respectively, surfacing significantly more data points to reveal meaningful trends.
